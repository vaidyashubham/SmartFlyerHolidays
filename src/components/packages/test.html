After breakfast at the hotel. We visit Bajra Shandhi Incl. entrance to 3D Interactive Art Museum, Bali Museum, Puputan Square and Tanah Lot Temple. Bali Museum is one of the amous Places to visit in Denpasar city collecting the ancient object related to the history of Bali Island generally.
